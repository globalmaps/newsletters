# Global Mapping Newsletter
The International Steering Committee for Global Mapping (ISCGM) published the Global Mapping Newsletter quarterly every year and reports the status of the activities of the ISCGM and the relevant field. The newsletter was distributed to the Members of the ISCGM and relevant organizations.
